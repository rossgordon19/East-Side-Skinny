Connect


"Cèst La Vie" - The title alone means “That’s Life.” A beautiful concept to explain where I have been mentally over the last couple of years. My previous albums have always talked about my struggle and life when things weren’t the best. This album is celebrating those adversities, while also showing you how far you can make it if you stay consistent and work hard.

I shot this album cover in the cut where I broke into my first apartment, smoked weed, sold dime bags, and talked shit with a lot of my homies who are working to make their lives better, or currently in the system.

To say I’m thankful is an understatement. Thank you God for always keeping me in your corner and guiding me in the right direction. Thank you to everybody who has supported me this far in my career. Thank you to the Eastend for giving me the game that I will continue to pass down to the young niggas that come after me. Last but not least, thank you to my OG, my Momma. Who would’ve thought? From repos to sliding on chrome- I Love You Forever.

- Skinny Mane
